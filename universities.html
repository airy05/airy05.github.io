Universities

  • Economy Kyiv National University of Technology and Design
    Kyiv, Ukraine
    (september 2016 - may 2018)

    At the Faculty of Economics of the KNUTD, I crossed a threshold that opened up another side of working on projects. It was an opening that gave me the opportunity to assess the prospects of projects and deepen my skills in working in a team and with customers. Here I learned the importance of project documentation and mastered concepts such as "privacy, data processing, rights protection, terms of use, supply and demand, project profitability, domestic and foreign market, advertising." This knowledge became an important part of my work in all areas, helping me to understand not only the technical aspects, but also the essence of the matter.

  • Design Kyiv National University of Technology and Design
    Kyiv, Ukraine
    (september 2014 - may 2016)

    At KNUTD, I continued my journey into the world of professional design.Here I had the opportunity to explore more deeply the methods of creating projects and expand my knowledge and skills. Studying at this faculty became the basis for my work in a team to implement joint educational projects and accompany them with appropriate documentation. The skills acquired here became indispensable in the further development of my path.

  • Design Cherkassy Art and Technical College
    Cherkassy, Ukraine
    (september 2010 - may 2014)

    DCATC laid the foundation for my understanding of project creation processes and provided me with the key fundamentals of design. Here I first set foot on the path that led me to the creation of aesthetic and functional objects. Studying at this college gave me the opportunity to master the basics of design, understand stylistic aspects, practical components, and the importance of usability. The skills acquired here have become my important advantage and resource on the path of development.
