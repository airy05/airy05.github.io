Hello!

My name is Iryna Chesnokova, I come from Ukraine, and I currently live in the Czech Republic. This page is my personal window into the world of web development and my way of learning and improving my skills.

At the university, I studied the art of creating visually attractive and functional projects of various directions - from cover and business card design to exteriors. My background in design has allowed me to understand things from both an aesthetic and a practical point of view. All these skills have only strengthened my interest in technology and the ability to create creative and interactive projects.

Later, my interest started leaning towards economics and business. Intrigued by the opportunity to comprehend market processes, I decided to broaden my horizons and obtain a degree in economics. My education in economics gave me an understanding of economic processes and the business world.

My development journey also includes work in the field of sales and banking, where I developed communication and cooperation skills with various clients. Yet it motivates and inspires me to independently acquire new technologies.

But life always leads to new opportunities and challenges. In connection with the war and Russia's military aggression against Ukraine, I was forced to change my place of residence and move to the Czech Republic. Here I had to change not only my place of residence, but also my professional path. I currently work in a job that doesn't match my development ambitions and goals, but it motivates and inspires me to independently acquire new technologies and develop my own career in web development.

At present, my educational and professional journey encompasses learning key web development technologies, including JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I don't just acquire theoretical knowledge; I also apply it in practice by developing my own web projects. These courses inspire me to find new web solutions and approach problems creatively and help me develop the technical skills that are necessary for web development.

My main goal is to become a successful web developer and realize my creative potential in this field. I believe that web development is the language of the future, and I am ready to contribute to the creation of visually appealing and functional web projects.

I trust that this brief glimpse into my life and professional journey has piqued your interest. For more detailed information about my achievements and projects, I invite you to familiarize yourself with the content of this site and share your thoughts and ideas with me.

Together we can achieve a lot!
